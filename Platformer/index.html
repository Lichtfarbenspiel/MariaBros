Super Maria Sis










Help Maria to get the golden gemstone.
Be aware of poisonous frogs that are living in these swamps.
On your way you can collect a few other things,
however be sure to choose wisely what you take with you.
For there might be other things that could harm you.
You can walk by pressing either A/D keys or left/right arrows.
Use the space key to jump and by pressing the W/arrow up key
you can fight back against the frogs.
